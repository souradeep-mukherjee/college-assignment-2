About Bhangarh Fort

Bhangarh Fort was built in the seventeenth century. This fort was built by Raja Madho Singh, younger brother of Man Singh. Raja Madho Singh was posted as a general in Akbar's army at that time. At that time the population of Bhangarh was about 10,000. Bhangarh is a magnificent fort located in Alwar district which has been built in a very huge size.
Surrounded by mountains on all sides, this fort has excellent sculptures. Apart from this, there are excellent and ancient temples of Lord Shiva, Hanuman etc. in this fort. There are a total of five gates in this fort and a main wall. Strong and sturdy stones have been used in this fort which have been lying in their original state since ancient times..
